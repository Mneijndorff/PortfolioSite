About PlanetRunner

PlanetRunner is an 2D addictive arcade game, the game is made for joy and frustration. The game is n endless runner. you play as a dinosaur on a planet that is being struck by meteors. you have to dodge the meteors by walking either clockwiseÂ or counter-clockwise. your score is based on time, and the longer you stay alive the harder it gets.

What did I do

The menu


You don't have to use the mouse to select an option. You can use the buttons W and S to move up and down. To select an option you use the R button. This menu is made with the use of index numbers and an offset Every number of the index is linked to a scene that gets loaded on trigger.

PowerUps

The ice powerup creates an ice layer around the world, this causes you to run faster until the ice melts and disappears. It will last for 8 seconds

The gun powerup gives you 10 bullets to shoot, the bullets will destroy the meteorites on impact.
The bullets will also stack. So if you have already collected a bullet powerup your ammo ammout will be added with 10.

The Shield powerup defends you agains the meteorites. When the shield is collected you can use it any time. it will stay for 8 seconds.

The High score


This is the high score mecanic, it saves the time you survived and compares this to the previeus high score
If the survived time is greater than the current highscore, the high score will be updated to the new high score.
It also saves the score when you close the application.
