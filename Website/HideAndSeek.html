About Hide And Seek

This is a Hide and Seek game. You have to hide from the enemy's. When they spot you, they will chase you. If when they lose you they will stop chasing and start searching for you. When they can't find you they will go back to patrolling. You can sneek behind them, when you are close to them and start walking they get suspicious. When you run close to them they will immediately start to chase you.
