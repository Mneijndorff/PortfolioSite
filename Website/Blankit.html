About 404 Not Found

404 not found is a 3D escape game. I worked on this game together with another developer and 4 artist. You are walking in the woods but you are getting followed by a evil spirit. This spirit will caught you and will trap you inside a room. Your objective is to escape the room you are in. You need to find different objects hidden in the room to complete steps.
